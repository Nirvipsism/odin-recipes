Lasagna Recipe

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is a family favorite and a comfort food staple. Layers of pasta, savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and gooey melted cheese come together to create a dish that's hearty, delicious, and perfect for any occasion.

Whether you're cooking for a crowd or preparing a cozy dinner at home, this lasagna is sure to impress. Serve it with a side of garlic bread and a fresh salad for the ultimate meal.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound Italian sausage
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef and sausage over medium heat. Drain any excess grease.
  4. Add the marinara sauce to the meat mixture. Simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan cheese, and parsley (if using).
  6. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  7. Layer 3 lasagna noodles over the sauce. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, then sprinkle 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese.
  8. Repeat the layers (sauce, noodles, ricotta, mozzarella) two more times.
  9. Finish with a layer of sauce and the remaining mozzarella cheese on top.
  10. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  11. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  12. Let the lasagna cool for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
